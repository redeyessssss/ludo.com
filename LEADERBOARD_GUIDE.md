## ğŸ“Š Understanding Ratings

### What is ELO?
ELO is a rating system that measures your skill level:
- **1000**: Starting rating (Beginner)
- **1200**: Intermediate player
- **1500**: Advanced player
- **1800**: Expert player
- **2000+**: Master player

### How Ratings Change
- **Beat higher-rated player**: Gain more points
- **Beat lower-rated player**: Gain fewer points
- **Lose to higher-rated player**: Lose fewer points
- **Lose to lower-rated player**: Lose more points

### Example:
```
You (1200) vs Opponent (1000)
- If you win: +8 rating
- If you lose: -24 rating

You (1000) vs Opponent (1200)
- If you win: +24 rating
- If you lose: -8 rating
```
